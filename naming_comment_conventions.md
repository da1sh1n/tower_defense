\# Naming Conventions:

* CONSTANTS
* ClassStructName
* functionName
* variable\_name
* file\_name
* \_private\_variable\_name

Note: `static` globals (mutable or not) follow the CONSTANTS convention (SCREAMING_SNAKE), same as `const` — both are module-level bindings, not locals. See `globals.rs`.
